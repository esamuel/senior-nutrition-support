‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start tracking my meals?
Tap the "+" button on the Nutrition tab, then use voice input or manual entry to add your meals. The app will automatically analyze nutritional content.
How do I set up medication reminders?
Go to the Medications tab, tap "Add Medication," enter your medication details, and set reminder times. The app will send notifications at scheduled times.
Can I use the app in my native language?
Yes! The app supports English, Spanish, French, and Hebrew. Change language in Settings > Language.
How do I upgrade to Advanced or Premium?
Tap any "Upgrade" button in the app, or go to Settings > Subscription. Advanced tier includes a 7-day free trial.
How do I export my health data?
This feature is available in Advanced and Premium tiers. Go to Health tab > Export Data to generate reports for your healthcare provider.
Is my data secure and private?
Yes, all data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share personal health information. Data syncs privately through your iCloud account.

üîß Troubleshooting

App Won't Open or Crashes

  • Force close the app and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Update to the latest app version
  • Ensure iOS 14.0 or later

Voice Recognition Not Working

  • Check microphone permissions in Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone
  • Ensure you're in a quiet environment
  • Try speaking clearly and at normal volume
  • Check your device's language settings

Notifications Not Appearing

  • Enable notifications in Settings > Notifications > Senior Nutrition App
  • Check Do Not Disturb settings
  • Ensure the app is not in Low Power Mode restrictions

Data Not Syncing

  • Check iCloud settings and ensure you're signed in
  • Verify internet connection
  • Try logging out and back into iCloud
